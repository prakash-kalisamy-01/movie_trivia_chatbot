Movies Trivia Knowledge Base
This document contains trivia facts about various movies, including details about directors, 
lead actors, release years, genres, and notable achievements. It serves as a knowledge 
base for the Movie Trivia Chatbot.
The Shawshank Redemption (1994)
- Director: Frank Darabont
- Lead Actors: Tim Robbins, Morgan Freeman
- Release Year: 1994
- Genre: Drama
- Trivia: Initially a box office flop, it gained a cult following through home video releases. 
Nominated for seven Academy Awards, including Best Picture.
Pulp Fiction (1994)
- Director: Quentin Tarantino
- Lead Actors: John Travolta, Samuel L. Jackson, Uma Thurman
- Release Year: 1994
- Genre: Crime/Thriller
- Trivia: Known for its non-linear narrative and iconic dance scene. Won the Palme d’Or at 
Cannes and an Oscar for Best Original Screenplay.
The Matrix (1999)
- Director: The Wachowskis
- Lead Actors: Keanu Reeves, Laurence Fishburne, Carrie-Anne Moss
- Release Year: 1999
- Genre: Science Fiction/Action
- Trivia: Pioneered the "bullet time" visual effect, revolutionizing action filmmaking. 
Grossed over $460 million worldwide.
Spirited Away (2001)
- Director: Hayao Miyazaki
- Lead Actors: Rumi Hiiragi (voice), Miyu Irino (voice)
- Release Year: 2001
- Genre: Animated Fantasy
- Trivia: First anime film to win an Academy Award for Best Animated Feature. Produced by 
Studio Ghibli, it’s Japan’s highest-grossing film of its time
